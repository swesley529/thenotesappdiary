Welcome to My Diary📝
What started off as lunch-hour conversations about controversial topics and hot takes with my college friends turned into a website to publicly showcase my impressions of these issues. As we listed our arguments, I documented them in my iPhone’s Notes app, hence the name of the website. Inspired by Sex and The City, Carrie Bradshaw – a NYC newspaper columnist – motivated me to publicize my research and reflections upon divisive matters. For those who engage with my essays, I don’t want you to solely agree with me. My intention with this website is to motivate others to expand their knowledge and for them to generate their own beliefs!!
